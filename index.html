About Us

The Kitchener-Waterloo Society for Innovation in Medicine is a group of healthcare and STEM students united by a shared passion for medical technology.
Our tenets...


To unite people from medical and STEM backgrounds for meaningful discourse


To empower students to be a driving force in the direction of medical technology


To collaborate with local initiatives to identify and solve real world problems

Our Members

Our members include students from the McMaster Medical School, Waterloo School of Pharmacy and the University of Waterloo Biomedical Engineering Program.
